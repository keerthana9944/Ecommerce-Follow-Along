# Ecommerce-Follow-Along

## Milestone 1: Overview
This e-commerce application provides a seamless online shopping experience with essential features for customers, administrators, and vendors. The application includes product browsing, order management, payment processing, and user authentication.

## Features

### 1. User Management
- **User Registration & Login**: Users can sign up and log in using email and password.
- **Profile Management**: Users can update personal details, addresses, and preferences.
- **Role-based Access**: Different dashboards for customers, vendors, and administrators.

### 2. Product Management
- **Product Catalog**: Displays a variety of products with images, descriptions, and prices.
- **Product Search & Filters**: Users can search and filter products by categories, price, and ratings.
- **Product Reviews & Ratings**: Customers can leave feedback and rate products.

### 3. Shopping Cart & Checkout
- **Add to Cart**: Users can add or remove items from their cart.
- **Wishlist**: Save products for future purchases.
- **Checkout Process**: Secure and seamless order placement.
- **Discount Coupons & Offers**: Apply promo codes for discounts.

### 4. Order Management
- **Order Tracking**: Real-time order status updates.
- **Order History**: View past orders and invoices.
- **Returns & Refunds**: Easy return process and refund management.

### 5. Payment Gateway
- **Multiple Payment Methods**: Supports credit/debit cards, PayPal, UPI, and net banking.
- **Secure Transactions**: Ensures encrypted payment processing.

### 6. Shipping & Delivery
- **Shipping Options**: Standard, express, and international shipping.
- **Delivery Tracking**: Real-time tracking and estimated delivery time.

### 7. Vendor Management
- **Vendor Registration**: Sellers can register and list their products.
- **Inventory Management**: Vendors can manage stock levels and product details.
- **Sales Reports**: Generate reports on orders and revenue.

### 8. Admin Dashboard
- **User & Order Management**: Monitor users and process orders.
- **Product Approval**: Approve or reject vendor-listed products.
- **Reports & Analytics**: Track sales performance and customer behavior.

### 9. Customer Support
- **Live Chat & FAQs**: 24/7 customer support via chat.
- **Help Center**: FAQs and support tickets for issue resolution.

## Technologies Used
- **Frontend**: React.js
- **Backend**: Node.js 
- **Database**: MongoDB
- **Payment Gateway**: Stripe / Razorpay / PayPal
